## 5. Emergent Time — Thermal Time Hypothesis

### Current Status (2024-2026)

**Thermal Time Hypothesis** (Connes & Rovelli, 1994):
- Time emerges from thermal equilibrium states in a fundamentally timeless ontology
- Uses C*-algebraic modular automorphism groups to define time flow

**Recent analysis** (arXiv:2407.18948, Chua 2024):
- **Criticism**: TTH is circular — requires time to define equilibrium
- Rovelli's "timeless equilibrium" definition fails (neither necessary nor sufficient)
- Modular group doesn't automatically have dynamical meaning
- Works only in special cases (Rindler wedge, uniformly accelerating observers)

**Verdict**: **Conceptually unresolved** — promising but requires justification for dynamical interpretation without presupposing time.
