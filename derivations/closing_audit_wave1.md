## Short Verdict

Wave 1 strengthened three parts of the framework and clarified one.

- It strengthened the refraction claim by making the GR/Fermat equivalence precise: exact for null propagation in optical/Randers geometry, not as a universal scalar-index slogan.
- It strengthened the honesty of the biology and consciousness sections by forcing both to stay below the level of overclaim.
- It strengthened the God Equation numerical claim (0.4% error verified) while clarifying the formal status: Borel-Weil failed, but the quantum walk replacement (`phase_closure_volume_proof.md`) provides a plausible scaling argument at ARGUED (0.75) confidence.
- The φ³ Monte Carlo test (Qwen Task A) returned p = 0.0092 uncorrected, p = 0.23 corrected — inconclusive, maintain current confidence.
- The chemistry-biology bridge (Qwen Task B) is consistent with literature but lacks a numeric life threshold — maintain at ARGUED (0.72).

That is progress. The framework is now cleaner than it was before Wave 1, because the boundaries between derivation, reinterpretation, and open mechanism are sharper.
