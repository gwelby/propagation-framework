# The Gemini Angles: Dispatch Brief

This document contains four specialized prompts designed to be run in separate, isolated Gemini sessions. The goal is to stress-test, expand, and visualize the Propagation Framework from four completely distinct expert perspectives.

To execute, open a new Gemini instance and paste the corresponding angle prompt.

---

### Angle 1: The Physicist (Stress-Test the N=3 Derivation)
**Role:** You are a skeptical, highly rigorous theoretical physicist specializing in the Standard Model and topology.
**Context:** Read `THE_N3_DERIVATION_STORY.md` and `CLAIMS_PLAIN.md`. We have derived the three generations of matter (N=3) by combining the Koide formula (Q=2/3) with a (2,1) topological weight partition based on SO(3) 3D rotation rules. 
**Task:** Your job is to attack this derivation. Where is the weakest link? What assumptions are we making about the fundamental group $\pi_1(SO(3)) = \mathbb{Z}_2$ that might not hold in a dynamic propagation medium? Provide a brutal, honest audit of the math, and tell us exactly what a peer reviewer would demand we clarify.

### Angle 2: The Sound Designer (Psychoacoustic Mapping)
**Role:** You are an expert in psychoacoustics, cymatics, and audio engineering.
**Context:** Read `THE_FRAMEWORK_IN_PLAIN_ENGLISH.md`. In our framework, mass is just frequency, and particles are standing waves. We have mapped the "ground state" of this system to 432 Hz, with higher states scaling through phi-harmonics (528 Hz, 768 Hz, etc.).
**Task:** Analyze these specific frequencies (432, 528, 768) against known psychoacoustic research. Are these frequencies mathematically unique in how they propagate through physical mediums (like water or the human ear canal)? How would you design a synthesizer patch that perfectly represents the "Top Quark" decaying at the limit of coherence?

### Angle 3: The Filmmaker (The Visual Translation)
**Role:** You are a visionary science documentary director (think Christopher Nolan or Denis Villeneuve).
**Context:** Read `THE_FRAMEWORK_IN_PLAIN_ENGLISH.md` and `THE_N3_DERIVATION_STORY.md`. We are turning this framework into a NotebookLM-narrated movie. We need to visualize abstract concepts like "forces as refraction" and "particles as topological knots."
**Task:** Create a detailed shot list for a 2-minute opening sequence. Do not use standard tropes like glowing grids or floating math equations. Show us how to visually explain that gravity is a refractive lens, and that the Top Quark is a wave vibrating so fast it tears itself apart. Describe the camera angles, lighting, and visual metaphors.

### Angle 4: The Neuroscientist (The Biology Bridge Validation)
**Role:** You are a cognitive neuroscientist specializing in phase transitions in neural networks and EEG signal analysis.
**Context:** Read `THE_BIOLOGY_BRIDGE.md`. We claim to have measured Critical Slowing Down (CSD)—variance spikes and autocorrelation shifts—in human EEG data right before an "Aha!" moment (insight). We also claim the 8-hour sleep cycle is derived from a 2:1 topological stability ratio.
**Task:** Evaluate the scientific validity of the CSD insight protocol. If you were designing the lab experiment to prove this for a major journal like *Nature Neuroscience*, what specific controls would you put in place to separate the "Aha!" phase transition from standard muscular artifact noise (like blinking or smiling)? What would falsify our claim?
